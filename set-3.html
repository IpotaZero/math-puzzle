'f:X \rightarrow Y:'写像
'A,A_1,A_2 \subset X'

3.1 ☆☆☆

'A_1 \subset A_2'
Claim: 'f(A_1) \subset f(A_2)'
証明 [Claim: 'f(A_1) \subset f(A_2)' i.e. '\forall y \in f(A_1), y \in f(A_2)'] 'y \in f(A_1)'をとる
['y \in f(A_2)' i.e. '\exists x \in A_2; y = f(x)'] 'y \in f(A_1)'だから '\exists x \in A_1; y = f(x)'
'x \in A_1 \subset A_2'だから 'x \in A_2'
['y = f(x)'] 'x'の取り方より 'y = f(x)'

3.2 ☆☆☆

'A_1 \subset A_2'

証明 [Claim: 'f(A_1 \cup A_2) = f(A_1) \cup f(A_2)'] ['\subset: \forall y \in f(A_1 \cup A_2), y \in f(A_1) \cup f(A_2)'] y \in f(A_1 \cup A_2)
[y \in f(A_1) \cup f(A_2)]
