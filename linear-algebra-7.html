(2)

'\varphi:(W/\im f)^* \rightarrow \ker f^* ; h \rightarrow \-{h}'

['\varphi(h_0+h_1) = \varphi(h_0)+\varphi(h_1)' i.e. '\forall w \in W, \varphi(h_0+h_1)(w) = (\varphi(h_0)+\varphi(h_1))(w)'] 'w \in W'をとる

よって'\varphi(ch)(w) = (c\varphi(h))(w)' ['\varphi'は全単射] ['\varphi'は全射 i.e. '\forall w \in \ker f^*, \exists h \in (W / \im f)^* ; w^* = \varphi(h)'] 'w \in \ker f^*'をとる
['\exists h \in (W / \im f)^* ; w^* = \varphi(h)'] 'n = \dim W'とする '(u_1,...,u_n)'を'W'の基底とする '([u_1],...,[u_m])' は 'W/\im f'の基底である
